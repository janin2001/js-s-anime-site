ZEREF DRAGNEEL

"Zeref Dragneel is one of the two main antagonists turned anti-hero of the anime/manga series Fairy Tail, alongside Acnologia. He is considered the strongest mage in the series, as he possesses incredibly deadly, powerful, and destructive magic.Is Zeref a good guy? Zeref is said to have been the darkest, most evil Mage in the history of the Magic World, mastering the Black Arts and creating many Demons, some of which still wreak havoc in the present. "

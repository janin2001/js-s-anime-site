MEGUMI FUSHIGURO

"Megumi Fushiguro is a fictional character of the manga series Jujutsu Kaisen created by Gege Akutami. He inherited the prized Ten Shadows Technique of the Zenin clan and is favored by the strongest sorcerer himself. Satoru believes that Megumi could become as strong as he is and has stated he likely possesses even more skill and potential than Yuji. Mai Zenin commended him as a genius who started school ranked grade 2. "
